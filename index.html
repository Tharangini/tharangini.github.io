My Projects

Comparing Typicality Ratings between Human and Model Representations for Images

  • Compiled a dataset for the selected domains (Fish, Crabs, Mushrooms, Dinosaurs, Couches)
  • Compared the typicality ratings of each image of the domain by Humans, Convolutional Networks, and Vision Transformers (ViTs).
  • Convolutional Networks were not as successful as ViTs in typicality prediction. ViTs model the human mind in 3 out of the 5 domains
  • Tools used: Python, PyTorch, ResNet, Visual Transfomer



Risk Prediction Models for Diabetes Using Diabetes Health Indicators

 

  • Processed a health-related telephone survey that is collected annually by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention. Developed hypotheses based on the parameters involved and perform statistical modeling.
  • Conducted a literature survey to build a network to assess risk factors that are most predictive of diabetes risk and make accurate predictions of whether an individual has diabetes.
  • Used: Python, PyTorch



Realistic Face Rendering for 3D Mixed Reality Experience

  • Conceptualized a deep learning pipeline for time-series analysis of video communication data to perform an in-depth research project.
  • Devised a bounding box to detect the Virtual Reality (VR) headsets in the video captures and creates a realistic model of the user's face and use it to replace the part covered by the headset, enabling mixed reality experience. The accuracy of the prototype was 79%.
  • Used: Python, PyTorch, GAN



Speaker Identification of Whispered Speech using Neural Networks

  • Created a novel dataset of whispered audios of 30 people. Compared and contrasted classification techniques (SVM, Ensemble Models, CNN) on the dataset. Developed a system to utilize extracted features to identify the speaker and achieved an accuracy of 92%.
  • Used: Python
